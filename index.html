Approach

"The best way to find out if you can trust somebody is to trust them."

— Ernest Hemingway

This site embodies a philosophy of systematic experimentation. Rather than prescribing universal solutions, it documents what works in specific contexts. The goal is not to convince but to contribute useful data points to the collective understanding.

Each area of focus represents an ongoing experiment. Ideas are tested, refined, and sometimes discarded. What remains is what proves useful in practice, not just in theory.
