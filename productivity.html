Productivity

What works for me

Productivity is really important! It allows you to change your life in meaningful ways. A more productive person will generally live a better life than a less productive person. I've spent a lot of time brainstorming ways to make myself more productive. My suggestions are based on my own experiences, and I'm happy to hear feedback.

Suggestions

First, I cannot recommend enough the Cold Turkey Blocker, an application on Mac. This has probably saved me hundreds of hours of doomscrolling. Basically, it requests access to your entire mac, and then it can do things like close your browser, or distracting apps. During a blocked period, you literally cannot use entertainment. If you remove the chrome extension that blocks youtube, chrome is continuously quit until you re-enable it. It continues to work even when you quit the application.
