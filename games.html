What I'm playing

Here are the games I'm currently playing and enjoying.

Northgard

A Viking-themed RTS that I've been really into lately. I usually play against my brother in the duel mode. There's something satisfying about like keeping track of your village and figuring out the fastest way to develop.

Minecraft Bedwars

My go-to game when I have a limited amount of time and my brother is willing to play. There's something addictive about the fast-paced PvP combined with the strategic components. Figuring out whether to go to the diamond gen straight away or get a stone sword before, stuff like that. I've gotten pretty decent at duels with my brother, often to the point where he only wants to play with a friend on his team. We use bedwarspractice.club.

Zelda

I'm replaying Breath of the Wild since the Switch 2 upgrade just came out. It's a really nice relaxing game. The physics and visual aesthetics are amazing. I often struggle to decide between helping the poor villagers with their issues and making actual progress.
